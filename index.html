About

 Learn More >

This project encourages you to examine the confluence between cafés, the urban environment, and the creativity of multilingual Jewish communities. The mapping and digital storytelling tools you will find here are based on extensive research done for Prof. Shachar Pinsker’s book A Rich Brew: How Cafés Created Modern Jewish Culture (NYU Press, 2018). Consider this site a companion resource, or a standalone project for you to experience.

Explore

You can examine Jewish café culture through three different lenses: cities, people, and time. The first option allows you to explore the interconnected network of cafés geographically. In the second option, you can learn more about the writers, artists, journalists, activists, and actors who were connected through cafés. Finally, you can view a timeline of the cafés established from the early 19th century to present.
